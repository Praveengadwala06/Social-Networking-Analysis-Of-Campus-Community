Courses We Offer

We are offer to three best computer science course for student by College

Data Science

Data Science course is a full time four years (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer science.This degree is mainly focused on Data science combines math and statistics, specialized programming, advanced analytics, artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning

Internet of things(iot)

internet of things course is a full time four years (eight semesters) Bachelor’s Degree in Computer science. IoT is basically about storing, processing, securing, and managing information. This degree is primarily focused on subjects such as software, databases, and networking.

Cyber Security

cs course is a full time four years bachealor's degree in computer science.The Cyber Security course teaches the students how to identify the computer system vulnerabilities, to recognise digital exploitation and also prevent damage such as loss of data, loss of money through viruses
