Clubs

Algorand blockchain club:

Blockchain Club (ABCs): is a global initiative by the Algorand Foundation tied up with GNITS to build a community of students interested in blockchain technology.
Objectives: Training students to build their skills to develop projects in blockchain technology.
Help students to establish networking and work collaborated with industry and fellow student community working in blockchain which helps in building their careers.
Mission:- Empowering students to get well-versed in developing decentralized, transparent, and efficient secured data transactional solutions using blockchain technology for building a network of trust, innovation, and inclusivity, reshaping industries, and enhancing collaboration for a decentralized future to make them future-ready.
Vision:- To foster a dynamic and inclusive community dedicated to exploring, understanding, and leveraging the potential of blockchain technology by empowering students with the required knowledge and skillset needed to build a career in the same.
